Unity Golf Foundation

Democratizing golf access for underserved youth, seniors, veterans, and justice-involved individuals.

Our Mission

Unity Golf Foundation exists to break barriers and build community through golf. We believe everyone deserves access to the sport, the mentorship, and the transformative experience that golf provides, regardless of background or circumstance.

What We Do

Third Home Community

Creating welcoming, affordable spaces where individuals find belonging, mentorship, and opportunity to develop both on and off the course.

Affordable Programming

Providing access to golf instruction, equipment, and play at costs that work for underserved communities, removing financial barriers to participation.

Mentorship & Growth

Pairing participants with experienced mentors who guide personal development, resilience, and life skills alongside golf fundamentals.
